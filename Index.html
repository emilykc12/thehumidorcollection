About Us

The Humidor Collection is an at-home local embroidery and sewing service, operated right from our unique boat-home in Charleston, SC. We offer everything from simple pocket tee embroidery to custom curtains for your home, with prices ranging from $10 to $300. Whether it's personalized apparel or bespoke upholstery, we bring a nautical touch to every stitch.

Our Services

  • Pocket Tees: Custom embroidery on tees for a personal touch.
  • Polos: Professional designs on polo shirts.
  • Bags & Hats: Embroider your accessories with style.
  • Any Type of Clothing: From jackets to jeans, we customize it all.
  • Custom Upholstery: Tailored curtains, cushions, and more for your home or boat.
